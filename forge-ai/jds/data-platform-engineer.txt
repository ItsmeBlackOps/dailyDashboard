Senior Data Platform Engineer
Instacart · Remote (US)

About the role
We are hiring a Senior Data Platform Engineer to join the Core Data team. You
will own the ingestion, transformation, and serving layer that powers analytics
and ML at Instacart — moving tens of billions of events per day through a
batch + streaming stack. The role is heavily backend and infra-focused; you
will spend most of your time in Python, SQL, and Airflow DAGs.

What you'll do
- Design and run petabyte-scale data pipelines on Spark + Airflow
- Own our dbt project — 1,200+ models across finance, growth, and logistics marts
- Build and maintain streaming pipelines in Kafka + Flink for near-real-time features
- Partner with ML engineers to productionize feature stores and backfills
- Lead migrations — we are midway through a Redshift → Snowflake cutover
- Write design docs, run architecture reviews, mentor mid-level data engineers

What we're looking for
Must-have:
- 5+ years building data platforms in production
- Strong Python and SQL — you have written and debugged thousand-line DAGs
- Deep Airflow experience (custom operators, SLAs, backfills)
- Spark / PySpark at scale (batch, not just notebooks)
- Snowflake or equivalent (Redshift, BigQuery) — partitioning, clustering, cost tuning
- dbt in production (not just a POC)
- Kafka in production

Nice to have:
- Flink / streaming ETL
- Terraform / infra as code
- Iceberg or Delta Lake
- Feature store experience (Feast, Tecton)
- Prior fintech, logistics, or marketplace background

Culture
We document before we code. We value engineers who can own an incident end to
end and write a postmortem that a non-technical PM can read. Remote-first with
quarterly offsites in SF.
