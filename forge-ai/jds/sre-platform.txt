Site Reliability Engineer — Platform
Shopify · Toronto (Hybrid)

About the role
SRE on the Platform team at Shopify. You are responsible for the reliability,
scalability, and cost-efficiency of the services that hundreds of product teams
build on top of. Black Friday / Cyber Monday is our World Cup — the platform
has to absorb 30x normal traffic without drama.

What you'll do
- Run incident response for tier-0 services; author postmortems that change behavior
- Build tooling for capacity planning, load testing, and chaos engineering
- Own SLIs / SLOs / error budgets across 40+ critical services
- Reduce toil — if it's done twice by hand, automate it the third time
- Partner with product teams on reliability reviews before launches
- Contribute to our Kubernetes platform (internal PaaS)

What we're looking for
Must-have:
- 4+ years in SRE, Platform, or Infra engineering
- Strong Go or Python for tooling
- Production Kubernetes — not just using it, extending it (operators, CRDs, admission)
- Prometheus, Grafana, and alerting at scale
- Deep Linux systems knowledge (networking, cgroups, tracing)
- Incident response experience — you have been on-call for a tier-0 system
- Terraform or equivalent infra-as-code

Nice to have:
- Ruby / Rails background (Shopify is largely Rails)
- Chaos engineering (Litmus, Gremlin)
- eBPF-based observability
- Cost optimization work on AWS or GCP
- Prior e-commerce or high-traffic retail experience

Culture
We value calm under pressure, strong writing, and humility. We do not hire for
hero culture. If you escalate early and document clearly, you'll fit in.
