Staff Software Engineer — Distributed Systems
Cloudflare · Remote (Americas)

About the role
We are hiring a Staff Engineer to own the architecture of our global control
plane. This service coordinates configuration rollout across 300+ data centers
with sub-second convergence. You will set direction across 3 teams and be
deeply involved in system design, code review, and mentorship.

What you'll do
- Drive multi-quarter architecture for the control plane and its dependencies
- Lead design reviews for changes that touch cross-team data contracts
- Own reliability targets — 99.99% availability, p99 config propagation < 800ms
- Partner with Product on tradeoffs between safety and feature velocity
- Mentor senior engineers; grow the next wave of tech leads
- Influence company-wide patterns for consistency and failure isolation

What we're looking for
Must-have:
- 8+ years building distributed systems in production
- Go or Rust (we run both; most new services are in Go)
- Deep experience with consensus / coordination (Raft, Paxos, etcd, ZooKeeper)
- Strong grounding in failure modes — split brain, thundering herd, retry storms
- Production Kubernetes at multi-cluster scale
- Track record of leading multi-team technical initiatives
- Written design docs that shipped

Nice to have:
- Prior work on edge compute, CDN, or networking
- gRPC + Protobuf at scale
- Experience with eBPF, kernel networking, or QUIC
- Open-source contributions to infrastructure projects

Culture
We prefer engineers who think in terms of failure first. We disagree in public,
write things down, and move on. The Staff bar is "can operate ahead of the
roadmap and bring people with you."
